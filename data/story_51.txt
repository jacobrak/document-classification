“But she wept not; she only kept repeating:
‘O my friend! O my brother!’
“‘Then you will go with me everywhere,’
he said to her, some fifteen minutes later, as
before enclosing and supporting her in his
embrace.
“‘Everywhere, to the end of the earth; wherever
you are, there shall I be.’
“‘And you are sure you do not deceive yourself?
 You know your parents will never consent
to our marriage?’
“‘I am not deceiving myself; I know it.’
