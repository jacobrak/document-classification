“‘But now … But now I must go. Good-by!’
“Had Insarof at this moment raised his eyes
to Helen, he would have seen that her whole face
shone,—shone the more, the more his face
grew gloomy and dark; but his eyes were stubbornly
fixed on the floor.
“‘Well, good-by, Dimitry Nikanorovitch,’ she
began. ‘But since we have met, give me now at
least your hand.’
“Insarof started to give her his hand. ‘No,
I cannot even do that,’ he said, and again turned
away.
