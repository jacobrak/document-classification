“And art thou ready even for—crime?”
The girl dropped her head.
“Yes, even for crime am I ready.”
The voice renewed not its questionings forthwith.
“Knowest thou,” spake the voice for the last
time, “that thou mayest be disenchanted in thy
ideals, that thou yet mayest come to see that
thou wert misguided, and that thy young life
has been wasted in vain?”
