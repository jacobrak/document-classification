“‘Stand still, stand still; just let me get a
good look at you,’ he continued, as he turned
them about. ‘What long jackets you have on!
What a jacket! Who ever heard of such jackets
before! Just let one of you take a run, and
see whether he would not tumble over, entangled
in his coat-tails.’
“‘Don't laugh, father, don't laugh,’ said at
last the eldest.
“‘See how touchy he is! And why, pray,
shall not I laugh?’
“‘Because! For even if you are my father,
but if you laugh, by God, I will thrash you!’
“‘Well, well, well, did you ever! Is this the
kind of a son you are? How? Your father?’
said Taras Bulba, stepping back in surprise.
