“‘You know I am poor, almost a beggar?’
“‘I know it.’
“‘That I am not a Russian, that I am fated

break all your ties with your country and your
family?’
“‘I know it, I know it.’
“‘You know also that I have devoted my life
to a difficult, thankless task; that I … that
we shall have to expose ourselves not only to
dangers, but to deprivation, and to degradation
perhaps?’
