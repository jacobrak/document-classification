dying person.
“‘Ah! I am still … alive,’ he said with a
hardly audible voice. ‘Unsuccessful as ever.…
I am detaining you.’
“‘Aliosha,’ Marianne contrived to groan out.

flowers.” … Where then are the flowers?…
But you are here instead … there, in
my letter.…’ Suddenly he began to shiver
from head to foot.
